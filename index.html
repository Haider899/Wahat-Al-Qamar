About Us

Leading Trailer Base Manufacturer in the UAE

WAHAT AL QAMAR is a Dubai-based manufacturer specializing in trailer bases, chassis assemblies, and complete trailer solutions for the construction, precast, oil & gas, and logistics sectors.

Large enough to deliver volume production at competitive rates — yet agile enough to engineer bespoke designs, modify existing fleets, and prototype next-generation trailer platforms for your project.

  • ADR / GCC road-compliant builds
